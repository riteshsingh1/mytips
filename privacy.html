Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

MyTips respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the MyTips mobile application.

Information We Collect

MyTips does not require you to create an account. Any information you enter in the app, such as shifts, hours, tips, and notes, is stored locally on your device and is used only to provide app functionality.

Automatically Collected Information

MyTips does not collect personal identifiers, location data, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or tracking data.

In-App Purchases

MyTips offers optional in-app purchases using Apple’s In-App Purchase system. Payments are processed securely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. Purchase status is used only to unlock Pro features.

Analytics

MyTips may collect basic app performance or crash data to improve app stability. This data does not personally identify users and is not used for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Sharing

MyTips does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Security

Your data remains on your device and is protected by your device’s security features. We take reasonable measures to ensure the app operates securely.

Children’s Privacy

MyTips is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
