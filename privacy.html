Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

MyTips ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Tip and shift data (dates, hours, cash tips, card tips, payouts, notes)
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No account creation or personal identification required

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (operating system, device model)
  • Usage data (app functionality, features used)
  • This information is collected automatically through standard app functionality

2.3 iCloud Sync (Optional)

  • If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • iCloud sync is optional and can be disabled at any time

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process and store your tip and shift data locally on your device
  • Enable optional iCloud sync across your Apple devices (when enabled)
  • Generate statistics and reports based on your data
  • Provide customer support

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Local Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage
  • Data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
  • You can export your data as CSV files at any time

4.2 iCloud Storage

  • If iCloud sync is enabled, data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Data is encrypted by Apple's iCloud service
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data

4.3 Data Security

  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data
  • Local data is protected by your device's security features
  • iCloud data is protected by Apple's security measures

5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Track you across other apps or websites
  • Collect analytics or tracking data

Your data remains private and is only accessible to you.

6. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the App
  • Export your data as CSV files
  • Delete your data by clearing app data
  • Disable iCloud sync at any time
  • Uninstall the App, which removes all local data

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

8.1 Apple Services

  • The App uses Apple's iCloud service (optional) for data synchronization
  • iCloud is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • We do not control Apple's data practices

8.2 Export Functionality

  • CSV export uses your device's native sharing functionality
  • Data is shared only through your chosen sharing method (email, cloud storage, etc.)
  • We do not have access to data shared through export

9. DATA RETENTION

  • Data is retained as long as the App is installed on your device
  • If you uninstall the App, all local data is deleted
  • iCloud data persists according to your iCloud account settings
  • You can delete data at any time through the App

10. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information

12. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

  • Data stored locally on your device remains on your device
  • iCloud data may be stored in Apple's data centers worldwide
  • iCloud data transfers are subject to Apple's data transfer practices

13. CONTACT US

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: hello@imritesh.com

We will respond to your inquiry within a reasonable timeframe.

14. CONSENT

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

15. DATA DELETION

To delete your data:

  • Uninstall the App to remove all local data
  • Disable iCloud sync to stop syncing to iCloud
  • Delete iCloud data through your device's iCloud settings

16. NO DATA COLLECTION FOR ADVERTISING

We do not collect data for advertising purposes. The App does not display advertisements and does not share data with advertisers.

17. COMPLIANCE

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
